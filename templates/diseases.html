Prevention Strategies

Crop Rotation

Rotate potatoes with non-host crops for at least 3 years to reduce disease carryover in soil.

Proper Spacing

Ensure adequate spacing between plants to improve air circulation and reduce humidity.

Water Management

Water at the base of plants early in the day to allow foliage to dry quickly.

Regular Monitoring

Inspect plants weekly for early signs of disease and remove infected plants immediately.

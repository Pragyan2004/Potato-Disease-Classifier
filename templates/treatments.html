Organic Treatment Options

Copper Fungicides

Organic copper-based fungicides can help control both early and late blight when applied preventatively.

Bacillus Subtilis

This beneficial bacterium acts as a biological fungicide and is approved for organic use.

Neem Oil

Neem oil has antifungal properties and can help prevent the spread of fungal diseases.

Baking Soda Spray

A solution of baking soda and water can create an alkaline environment that inhibits fungal growth.

When to Consult an Expert

If your plants show any of the following signs, consider consulting with an agricultural expert:

  • Rapid spread of disease despite treatment
  • More than 50% of plants infected
  • Unidentified symptoms that don't match common diseases
  • Recurring infections year after year
